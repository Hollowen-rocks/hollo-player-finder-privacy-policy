Privacy Policy for Hollo's Player-Finder

1. Introduction

Hollo's Player-Finder ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a tool designed to enhance the Roblox gaming experience by helping players locate and join game servers more efficiently. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

2. Data Collection and Usage

Information We Collect:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or location.

Permissions Required:

  • activeTab: This permission allows the extension to access the currently active tab in your browser to interact with the Roblox website. It enables features like fetching game server information and displaying user thumbnails.
  • host permissions: Specifically for https://www.roblox.com/*, this permission is necessary to fetch data such as user profiles, game server details, and avatar thumbnails from Roblox APIs.
